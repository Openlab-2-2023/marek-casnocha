Archidon

"Our arrows will blot out the sun"

About:

Low health basic Light ranged unit
of the Order empire that is powerful in large numbers
especially against Light units
very powerful when combined with Heavy units such as
Speartons, Juggerknights, Rippriders

Current Stats:

Tags: Order Light
dps: 5/s
Damage: 10+5vs Light
Health: 70hp
Cost: 300 gold
Population: 3
Training time: 13seconds

Abilities:

Arrow cam (User control only!):
Once you shoot your arrow,
this ability will make your screen follow the arrow

Effective against:

Sicklewrath, Crawlers, Deads, Eclipsors
Giants, Magikills
note: being effective doesnt always mean they can win in a 1v1

Weak against:

Speartons, Juggerknights, Enslaved Giants, Shadowraths
Atreyos, Archis, Spearos, Kytchu, Bombers (all of these only while user controlled)
