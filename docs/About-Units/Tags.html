Tags

Each unit has a tag which is a part of their characteristics
Each tag has its own advantages and sometimes it comes with disadvantages (is shown below) and thanks to these tags you can for example train some units at the same time
current training queues are: Worker, Light, Heavy, Magic, General, Giants(if they have monstrosity upgrade lvl2)

Tags

Advantages

Disadvantages

ORDER

-can benefit from some stuff(Zarek´s training speed bonus, mana burst cooldown reduction) -dont have autoheal nor autocure unlike Chaos

CHAOS

-has autoheal (gives them passive +6hp every few seconds)
-has autocure(after some time they can cure poison from themselves without the need of healing spells)		 -doesnt get affected by Order bonus stuff (Mana burst, Zarek´s training speed bonus)

LIGHT

-gets affected by light-benefiting spells (rage for example)
-can be trained alongside worker, heavy, magic, general units

-Takes more damage from units that have bonus damage against light
-cannot be trained alongside other light units

HEAVY

-can be trained alongside other units
-benefits from Heavy healing wisp,Voltaic armor or other stuff that supports heavy units		 -cannot be trained alongside heavy units
-takes more damage from anti-heavy units

MAGIC

-can be trained alongside other units
-benefits from cost reduction from briliance 		-cannot be trained alongside other magic units

GENERAL

-usually very strong for their cost (at least theyre supposed to be)
-can be trained alongside every unit except another general
-when they die, after they get to base theyll respawn for free
-Insanely strong while User controlled correctly (If its a good general that is)
-stun time reduction		 -limit only 1 on the field
-when they die, they have to walk all the way back to base to respawn
-costs 9 population with a 50seconds training time

DEAD

-takes 50% less damage from poison
-benefits from rune of reanimation HP bonus
-benefit from acid rain movement speed boost
-Rune of Reanimation doesnt turn them into deads		 -enemy deads cannot be reanimated as deads with rune of reanimation

FLYING

-ground melee units cannot attack them -usually very easy to target with User Controlled ranged units

GIANT

-me big and strong
-me cannot be stunned
-me benefit from monstrosity upgrade 		-me big and slow
