Tags

Each unit has a tag which is a part of their characteristics
Each tag has its own advantages and sometimes it comes with disadvantages (is shown below) and thanks to these tags you can for example train some units at the same time or deal more damage to units with a certain tag
current training queues are: Worker, Light, Heavy, Magic, General, Giants(if they have monstrosity upgrade lvl2)

ORDER

Advantages

-can benefit from some stuff(Zarek´s training speed bonus, mana burst cooldown reduction)

Disadvantages

-dont have autoheal nor autocure unlike Chaos

CHAOS

Advantages

-has autoheal (gives them passive +6hp every few seconds)
-has autocure(after some time they can cure poison from themselves without the need of healing spells)

Disadvantages

-doesnt get affected by Order bonus stuff (Mana burst, Zarek´s training speed bonus)

LIGHT

Advantages

-gets affected by light-benefiting spells (rage for example)
-can be trained alongside worker, heavy, magic, general units

Disadvantages

-Takes more damage from units that have bonus damage against light
-cannot be trained alongside other light units

HEAVY

Advantages

-can be trained alongside other units
-benefits from Heavy healing wisp,Voltaic armor or other stuff that supports heavy units

Disadvantages

-cannot be trained alongside heavy units
-takes more damage from anti-heavy units

MAGIC

Advantages

-can be trained alongside other units
-benefits from cost reduction from briliance

Disadvantages

-cannot be trained alongside other magic units

GENERAL

Advantages

-usually very strong for their cost (at least theyre supposed to be)
-can be trained alongside every unit except another general
-when they die, after they get to base theyll respawn for free
-Insanely strong while User controlled correctly
-stun time reduction

Disadvantages

-limit only 1 on the field
-when they die, they have to walk all the way back to base to respawn
-costs 9 population with a 50seconds training time

DEAD

Advantages

-takes 50% less poison damage
-benefits from rune of reanimation HP bonus
-benefit from acid rain movement speed boost
-Rune of Reanimation doesnt turn them into deads

Disadvantages

Is there a one?

FLYING

Advantages

-ground melee units cannot attack them

Disadvantages

-usually very easy to target with User Controlled ranged units

GIANT

Advantages

-me big and strong
-me cannot be stunned
-me benefit from monstrosity upgrade

Disadvantages

-me am slow
