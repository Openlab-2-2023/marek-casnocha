Spearton

"Born to die, but not before them!"

About:

  • High health Heavy unit of the Order Empire
  • Isnt usually strong alone, needs other units to be very powerful
  • very powerful when combined with high dps Light units
  • helps to hold enemy units at the frontline
    preventing them from attacking backline units

Current stats:

  • Tags: Heavy Order
  • dps: 9+4.5 vs Light
  • dps while blocking: 4.5+2.25 vs Light
  • Damage: 10+5 vs Light -5 vs Spearos
  • Health: 450hp
  • cost 450 gold + 50 Crystal
  • population: 5
  • training time: 17seconds

Abilities:

Block:
Spearton gets into a block stance which reduces incoming damage by 3
at the cost of moving and attacking 50% slower

Effective against:

Sicklewrath, Crawlers, Archidons, Bombers

Weak against:

Swordwraths, Shadowraths, Giants, Magikills
Spearos, Enslaved Giants
note: being weak doesnt always mean they cant win in a 1v1
