Sicklewrath

"They will fall before they get a chance to stand"

About:

Sicklewrath is a basic Light melee unit of the Order empire
with low health and decent damage with splash against Light units
very weak against Heavy units
can become deadly when combined with tank units
such as Speartons, Juggerknights, Rippriders

Current stats:

tags: Order Light
dps: 5 +2vs Light /s
Damage: 5 +2vs Light
Splash dmg: 1 +2vs Light
Splash: 2 additional targets Health: 50hp
Cost: 75 gold
Population:2
training time: 10seconds

Abilities:

None

Effective against:

Crawlers, Deads, Giants(in small numbers)
note: being effective doesnt always mean they can win in a 1v1

Weak against:

Bombers(if you are not careful), Archidons, Magikills(especially with mana burst)
Enslaved Giants, Eclipsors, Speartons, Juggerknights, Rippriders
Rune of Reanimation(this can quickly turn your swordw into deads)
Voltaic enchants with lightning spells
