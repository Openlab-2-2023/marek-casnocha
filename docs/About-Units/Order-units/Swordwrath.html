Swordwrath

"Limbs will sever at mere sight!"

About:

  • Swordwrath is a basic Light melee unit of the Order empire
    with low health and high damage against Heavy units
  • very good at dealing with early Heavy units
  • can become deadly when combined with tank units
    such as Speartons, Juggerknights, Rippriders

Current stats:

  • tags: Order Light
  • dps: 8 +5vs Heavy/s
  • Damage: 8 +5vs Heavy
  • Health: 85hp
  • Cost: 125 gold
  • Population:3
  • training time: 11seconds

Abilities:

Leap:
Swordwrath performs a leap attack
dealing 12dmg and a 2 second stun on a single enemy unit
cooldown: 30seconds

Effective against:

Speartons, Juggerknights,Rippriders
Giants, Toxic Deads
note: being effective doesnt always mean they can win in a 1v1

Weak against:

Bombers, Archidons, Magikills
Enslaved Giants, Shadowraths, Eclipsors, Deads
